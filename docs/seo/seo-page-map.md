# SignalOps SEO Page Map

SignalOps helps small and mid-sized businesses stop missing leads by installing AI-powered lead response, qualification, follow-up, routing, and simple lead tracking systems.

Primary offer: **Free Lead Leak Audit**

Primary slogan: **Every lead answered. Every follow-up handled.**

## SEO Positioning

SignalOps should not sound like a generic AI software company. The SEO strategy should speak to owners and operators who are already getting leads but lose opportunities because calls, texts, forms, DMs, quote requests, and follow-ups are handled manually or inconsistently.

The strongest organic angle is practical revenue operations for small businesses:

- Faster response to new inquiries
- Missed-call text back
- Better qualification before the owner or sales rep follows up
- Follow-up reminders when customers do not book
- Simple tracking for businesses without a formal CRM
- Human handoff when AI is unsure, urgent, or risky

Local pages only make sense after SignalOps has a clear service area or case studies. Build these later and avoid thin city pages.

- `/locations/dallas-ai-lead-response`
- `/locations/fort-worth-ai-lead-response`
- `/locations/texas-ai-lead-automation`

Only build local pages when each can include local examples, testimonials, vertical focus, or a real sales motion.

## Page Template Rules

Each SEO page should follow a consistent structure while still feeling tailored:

1. Hero with one clear H1, direct problem statement, and CTA to the Free Lead Leak Audit.
2. Problem section focused on missed calls, slow replies, forgotten follow-ups, or weak qualification.
3. SignalOps workflow section showing capture, instant response, qualification, routing, follow-up, and dashboard visibility.
4. Industry or use-case examples with realistic calls, texts, forms, DMs, photo requests, or quote scenarios.
5. Example AI output, such as customer reply, internal note, lead score, tags, and recommended action.
6. FAQ section with skeptical business-owner questions.
7. Final CTA pointing to `/audit`.

## Content Rules

- Avoid generic AI fluff. Do not write as if AI alone solves the business.
- Use business-owner language: missed calls, slow replies, quote requests, texts, DMs, booking, follow-up, and lost opportunities.
- Always connect features to concrete outcomes: faster callback, cleaner handoff, better prioritization, fewer forgotten follow-ups.
- Include examples by industry instead of abstract promises.
- Include FAQs on every service, industry, comparison, and use-case page.
- Use screenshots, dashboard mockups, demo outputs, or generated lead qualification examples where possible.
- Always point to the Free Lead Leak Audit as the primary CTA.
- Avoid spammy city or industry pages with thin text swaps.
- Be conservative with claims. Use "helps," "designed to," and "can" instead of guaranteed revenue language.
- Mention human review for urgent, risky, regulated, or uncertain cases.
- Make pages useful even if the visitor does not buy.

## Internal Linking Rules

- Homepage should link to the top service pages, `/audit`, `/live-demo`, `/how-it-works`, `/roi-calculator`, and `/demo`.
- Every service page should link to at least two use-case pages and two industry pages.
- Every industry page should link to the most relevant service page, `/demo` if relevant, and `/audit`.
- Every use-case page should link to `/how-it-works`, `/audit`, and at least one industry page.
- Educational pages should link back to the relevant service and use-case pages.
- Comparison pages should link to `/how-it-works`, `/audit`, and a relevant service page.

## Implementation Notes for Codex

- Add page data as structured config where possible so metadata, FAQ schema, breadcrumbs, and internal links stay consistent.
- Use existing SEO helpers in `/lib/seo.ts`.
- Add new public pages to `app/sitemap.ts`.
- Add page metadata with unique title, description, canonical, OG, and Twitter fields.
- Add FAQPage schema when FAQ content exists.
- Keep `/dashboard` noindex unless it becomes a public SEO page.
- Keep CTAs consistent with the main offer: `Get a Free Lead Leak Audit`.
