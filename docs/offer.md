# SignalOps Offer

## Core Offer

SignalOps designs and implements AI-assisted lead operations systems for SMBs that rely on inbound inquiries, consultations, quotes, appointments, or sales calls.

## Phase 1: SignalOps Foundation

- Run a Free Missed Lead Check across calls, texts, forms, DMs, response paths, and handoffs
- Build an intake and qualification model
- Create response templates and routing rules
- Stand up reporting for lead volume, speed, score, and next action

## Phase 2: Automation Layer

- Connect CRM and calendar systems
- Add email and SMS follow-up sequences
- Configure appointment routing
- Create owner/operator visibility dashboards

## Phase 3: Optimization

- Review conversion data
- Tune qualification criteria
- Improve follow-up copy
- Identify channel-level bottlenecks

## Best-Fit Clients

- Local services and home services
- Healthcare and dental practices
- Professional services
- B2B service providers
- Appointment-based businesses with paid lead flow
