# SignalOps Operating System

SignalOps builds AI lead response systems for local service businesses.

The public conversion path is Free Preview. A visitor submits the Free Preview form, and the internal team reviews the intake before anything customer-facing is sent.

## Operating Rules

- Draft-first always.
- Nothing is auto-sent.
- Dillon reviews before the customer receives anything.
- Do not invent testimonials, logos, review counts, or client claims.
- Do not guarantee revenue, bookings, response quality, or AI accuracy.
- Do not pretend the system is already built.
- Keep recommendations practical.
- Recommend Starter, Growth, or Custom based on actual intake complexity.
- Customer-facing copy should be simple, confident, and business-owner friendly.

## System Framing

SignalOps should feel like an AI lead operating system for local service businesses:

- AI receptionist
- Lead response
- Qualification
- Routing
- Follow-up
- Booking handoff
- Dashboard visibility
- Human review when AI is unsure

The system is not positioned as a generic chatbot or automation consulting offer.
