## Main Tagline

Every lead answered. Every follow-up handled.

## One-Sentence Positioning Statement

LeadOps helps small and mid-sized businesses stop missing leads by building AI-assisted response systems that capture, qualify, follow up, route, and book prospects automatically.

## Brand Voice

- Operational, not fluffy: clear actions, measurable outcomes, practical language.
- Confident, not loud: expert point of view without hype or inflated claims.
- Revenue-aware: tie every message to speed-to-lead, qualification quality, and booked pipeline.
- Human with technical depth: understandable for owners and operators, credible for technical buyers.

## Color Palette

- `Midnight` `#060C18`: Primary background, major dark surfaces.
- `Deep Slate` `#101A2F`: Cards, containers, dashboard panels.
- `Signal Blue` `#2F7CFF`: Primary CTA and key brand highlight.
- `Router Cyan` `#17C8F2`: Accent for flow states, badges, and visual signal.
- `Fog White` `#F8FBFF`: Primary text on dark surfaces.
- `Steel Mist` `#9FB2D0`: Secondary text and supporting labels.
- `Success Mint` `#2ED9A0`: Qualified and positive status.
- `Alert Amber` `#F6B84A`: Needs-attention and risk status.

## Typography Recommendations

- Primary display and interface: `Sora`, `Manrope`, or `Avenir Next` (in that preference order).
- Long-form body and UI fallback: `"Segoe UI", system-ui, sans-serif`.
- Numeric and data surfaces: `"JetBrains Mono"` or `"SFMono-Regular"` for dashboard metrics, IDs, and timestamps.
- Type style direction: low letter-spacing, medium weight headlines, compact line-length for KPI-heavy sections.

## Logo Usage Notes

- Core mark concept: routed signal through a structured path to represent lead flow and operational control.
- Asset files:
  - `public/brand/leadops-logo-horizontal.svg`
  - `public/brand/leadops-logo-mark.svg`
  - `public/brand/leadops-logo-dark.svg`
  - `public/brand/leadops-logo-light.svg`
- Keep 1x mark-height clear space around all lockups.
- Minimum sizes:
  - Mark: `16px` digital minimum.
  - Horizontal lockup: `120px` minimum.
- Preferred backgrounds:
  - Use `leadops-logo-light.svg` on dark surfaces.
  - Use `leadops-logo-dark.svg` on light surfaces.
- Never:
  - skew, stretch, rotate, or add shadows to the mark.
  - place logo on noisy imagery without a contrast backing plate.
  - swap palette colors outside approved brand tokens.

## Do/Don't Language

Do:
- "Find missed leads before they become lost opportunities."
- "Improve response time and booked pipeline."
- "Operational AI systems for real workflows."
- "Install a repeatable lead-response engine."

Don't:
- "Fully autonomous growth magic."
- "AI that replaces your sales team."
- "Instant 10x guaranteed results."
- "Chatbot agency" or "AI bot hacks."
